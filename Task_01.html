Fashion Design








Courses

Pattern Making

Pattern making is the process of creating templates or patterns that are used to cut fabric pieces for garments. It involves drafting, draping, and altering patterns to fit the human body accurately.


Techniques


1. Flat Pattern Making

2. Draping

3. Digital Pattern Making




Sewing Techniques

Sewing techniques encompass the various methods and skills required to stitch fabric pieces together to create garments and accessories. Mastery of these techniques is fundamental for constructing durable and aesthetically pleasing items.


Techniques


1. Hand Sewing

2. Seam Finishes

3. Decorative Stitches


Textile Manipulation

Textile manipulation involves altering the surface and structure of fabrics to create unique textures, patterns, and forms. This can be done through various techniques that add artistic and functional value to the fabric.


Techniques


1. Fabric Dyeing

2. Fabric Printing

3. Surface Embellishment

More Information
A course in fashion designing offers a comprehensive and flexible way to explore the dynamic world of fashion. These courses typically cover a wide range of topics, including fashion illustration, pattern making, garment construction, textile selection, and the principles of design. Students gain practical skills through virtual workshops and interactive assignments, while also learning about the latest industry trends and technological advancements. Many courses are designed by experienced professionals, providing valuable insights and real-world knowledge. With the convenience of online learning, aspiring fashion designers can study at their own pace, balancing their education with other commitments, and preparing themselves for a successful career in the fashion industry.
